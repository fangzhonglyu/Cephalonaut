Cephalonaut

Lil Big Games

Alex Schindler, Angelina Saliling, Barry Lyu, Estelle Hooper, 
Michael Egbueze, Matias Goldfeld, Oliver Matte, Theodore Klausner

After a wormhole accident separates one nautical crew of explorers, Michael the octopus must
rescue his friends before they are lost in space forever!

Explore the phenomena of outer space in this a zero-gravity puzzles physics game.
As Michael the octopus, propel and swing through blackholes, wormholes and 
other extraterrestrial obstacles with your wits, tentacles, and ink sac thrusters.
Each world holds a new mission with a friend in need of saving!
